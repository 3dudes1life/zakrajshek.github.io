01 / The Story

This isn’t a reinvention.
It’s the full picture.

For years, most people knew me through Out at the Fair—the LGBTQ+ community program I helped grow into a nationally recognized event platform across California fairs.

But the work was always bigger than one event. It was about creating belonging in places where people didn’t expect to find it. It was storytelling, leadership, partnership, sponsorship, production, advocacy, and community design all at once.

Today, that same instinct powers my books, podcast, consulting, speaking, and every project still ahead. Zakrajshek.com is where those pieces finally live together.
