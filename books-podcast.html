Tres Amigos, Una Vida

Tres Amigos, Una Vida is a throuple love story inspired by true emotional terrain: vulnerability, devotion, conflict, healing and the work of building a life outside conventional templates. William is publicly identified as CEO and co-author.

A Little Throuple Tea

Hosted by William, Caleb and Daniel, A Little Throuple Tea is a weekly gay throuple video podcast covering relationship dynamics, family, money, travel, queer culture, humor and the everyday reality of a committed three-person partnership.
