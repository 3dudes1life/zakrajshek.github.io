# Zakrajshek.com Asset Checklist

Place final files in `assets/media/` using the exact filenames below. WebP is preferred for photography. Keep the original high-resolution files separately.

## Priority 1 — Homepage and identity

- `hero-portrait.webp` — vertical or square portrait, minimum 1600 px tall. Clean background; confident, current, warm. Used in the homepage hero.
- `og-home.jpg` — 1200 × 630 social-sharing graphic featuring your portrait, name and short positioning line.
- `william-caleb-daniel.webp` — horizontal photo of all three of you, minimum 2000 px wide. Current creative chapter.
- `signature.svg` — optional transparent vector or high-resolution PNG of your signature.

## Priority 2 — Proof and career archive

- `oatf-stage.webp` — wide crowd/stage image showing scale and LGBTQ+ visibility, minimum 2000 px wide.
- `oatf-founder.webp` — you at an Out at the Fair event, preferably with recognizable branding.
- `dj-will-z-action.webp` — you actively DJing; landscape, minimum 1800 px wide.
- `dj-will-z-archive-01.webp` through `dj-will-z-archive-06.webp` — flyers, club photos, app screenshots or press clippings. Scan at 300 dpi when applicable.
- `award-barham.webp` — clean award or recognition image.
- `award-40-under-40.webp` — official image, certificate or event photograph.
- `techstars.webp` — approved Techstars/OutAt cohort or founder image.

## Priority 3 — Books and podcast

- `throuple-tea-cover.webp` — official locked podcast artwork, square, at least 3000 × 3000.
- `podcast-studio.webp` — horizontal photograph of the three hosts recording.
- `book-1-cover.webp` and `book-2-cover.webp` — high-resolution flat front covers.
- `book-lifestyle.webp` — physical books photographed in a natural setting.
- `3dudes1life.webp` — a current lifestyle or creator image representing the broader brand.

## Priority 4 — Speaking and media kit

- `speaker-reel.mp4` — 60–120 second reel, 1080p minimum.
- `speaker-reel-poster.webp` — 16:9 still from the reel or strong stage photo, 1920 × 1080.
- `speaker-stage-01.webp` through `speaker-stage-04.webp` — you speaking, moderating or engaging an audience.
- `press-headshot.webp` — approved high-resolution portrait; neutral background and room to crop.
- `press-headshot-horizontal.webp` — horizontal alternative with negative space.
- `speaker-one-sheet.pdf` — polished one-page PDF with bio, topics, outcomes and contact information.
- `media-kit.zip` — approved bios, headshots, project art and contact information.

## Testimonials and written proof

Collect 4–8 short testimonials. For each one include: exact quote, full name, title, organization, written permission, and optional headshot/logo. Prioritize fair executives, community partners, media producers, speaking hosts and collaborators.

## Logos

Supply approved SVG or transparent PNG versions of OutAt Inc., Out at the Fair, A Little Throuple Tea, 3Dudes1Life Creative, and Tres Amigos, Una Vida. Do not use AI-remade versions of official logos.

## Business information needed

- Preferred public email address
- Speaking inquiry process and typical lead time
- Whether fees/budget ranges should be requested on the form
- Approved 50-, 100- and 250-word biographies
- Pronunciation guide for “Zakrajshek”
- Current social profile URLs you want publicly linked
- Any honors, awards or appearances missing from the timeline
- Privacy-friendly analytics provider and verification codes for Google Search Console/Bing Webmaster Tools
